# LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Objective
This project aims to analyze how B2B SaaS professionals use LinkedIn organic content to drive awareness, engagement, and pipeline growth.

## Why This Topic
LinkedIn has become a primary channel for B2B SaaS marketing, especially for founder-led growth and content-driven demand generation. Understanding how top practitioners use content strategically can provide actionable insights for building effective content systems.

## Methodology
This research collects and analyzes content from 10 selected experts in B2B SaaS, growth marketing, and LinkedIn strategy.

Sources include:
* LinkedIn posts
* YouTube content (where available)
* Additional materials (if relevant)

Each expert is selected based on:
* Active LinkedIn presence
* Proven experience in B2B SaaS or growth
* High-signal, insight-driven content (not generic motivation)

## Repository Structure
/research
sources.md → list of experts and rationale
/linkedin-posts → curated posts per expert
/youtube-transcripts → video transcripts (if available)
/other → additional materials

## Goal
The final goal is to identify patterns, frameworks, and best practices that can later be developed into a practical LinkedIn content playbook for B2B SaaS.
