### Key Insights

- High-quality B2B content is deeply intentional and iterative
  → Planning starts up to a year in advance  
  → Continuous refinement (reviewing past performance, rewriting, adjusting details)

- Content is a team effort, not individual output  
  → Execution involves multiple contributors, even if one person is the “face”  
  → Reflects how strong B2B content often represents collective thinking

- Obsession with details signals quality
  → Messaging, delivery, experience, and even small elements (like food, visuals, tone) matter  
  → Indicates that strong content = holistic experience, not just information

- Rewriting is core to content excellence
  → Speech rewritten multiple times  
  → Iteration is required, not optional

- Emotional connection matters in B2B
  → Focus shifts from logistics → audience experience  
  → Emphasis on gratitude, community, shared experience
