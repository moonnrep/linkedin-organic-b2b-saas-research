# Erin Balsa
Erin Balsa is a B2B SaaS content strategist known for building high-leverage content systems, not just individual posts.

## Post 1 — Testing Low-Ticket Consulting Offer
Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/erinbalsa_i-kicked-off-a-new-project-yesterday-a-share-7450146386492186624-FHsH

### Summary
Erin shares how she tested a new low-ticket consulting offer ($2,500) for B2B SaaS teams running research programs.

Instead of full-service execution, she positioned it as:
> “Do the work *with you*, not for you.”

### Insight
- Content can be used to **validate new offers publicly**
- LinkedIn becomes a **real-time GTM testing channel**
- Transparency builds trust and engagement

## Post 3 — B2B SaaS Content Playbook
Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/erinbalsa_if-i-were-leading-marketing-at-a-b2b-saas-share-7442194206938484736-caWO

### Summary
A full LinkedIn + GTM strategy built around research:
1. Build a strong POV  
2. Validate it with data  
3. Collaborate with experts  
4. Repurpose aggressively  
5. Promote winning content  
6. Connect to product experience  
7. Expand into offline channels  

### Insight
- LinkedIn content should come from a **central narrative**
- Repurposing must be **planned upfront**
- Organic content should feed:
  - Sales
  - Product
  - Events

## Post 4 — Credibility in Research Content
Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/erinbalsa_many-marketing-teams-publish-research-reports-activity-7440715265283260416-KNcF

### Summary
She criticizes poor research practices:
- Leading questions  
- Biased narratives  
- Misleading data interpretation  

Her stance:
> Credibility is more important than being agreeable.

### Insight
- Trust is a **competitive advantage in B2B SaaS**
- Honest data (even if imperfect) builds stronger authority
- Content = brand reputation

## Post 5 — Ungated Content Strategy
Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/erinbalsa_choosing-not-to-gate-your-report-is-a-bold-activity-7448329865499611136-jqyp

### Summary
Ungating content:
- Reduces attribution clarity  
- Increases reach and trust  

Case signal:
- Higher demo bookings  
- Stronger pipeline impact  
- Increased sales usage of content  

### Insight
- Not all ROI is directly measurable  
- Distribution and accessibility drive influence  
- LinkedIn benefits from **low-friction content access**
