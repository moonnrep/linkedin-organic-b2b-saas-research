# Ross Simmonds

## Overview
Ross Simmonds is the founder of Foundation, a content marketing agency focused on helping B2B SaaS companies generate pipeline through content.

His approach goes beyond content creation:
* Research → understanding audience, channels, and search behavior
* Create → producing multi-format content (blogs, social, video, Reddit, etc.)
* Distribute → repurposing and spreading content across platforms
* Optimize → improving performance via SEO, conversion, and data

Key philosophy:
> “Most agencies create content. We make it impossible to ignore.”

---

## Core Insight
Ross emphasizes that **content alone is not enough**.

The real advantage in B2B SaaS comes from:
* Distribution across multiple channels
* Consistent presence in platforms where buyers already search
* Long-term content investment that compounds over time

## Key Patterns Across Content
1. Distribution > Creation
   Content success depends on where and how it is distributed
2. Multi-channel presence
   Important platforms:
   * LinkedIn
   * Reddit
   * YouTube
   * Review sites
3. Value-first content
   * No hard selling
   * Focus on helping the audience
4. Long-term compounding
   * Results come from consistent effort over time
   * Not short-term tactics
