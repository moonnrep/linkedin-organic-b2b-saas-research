# Shraddha Shrivastava
### 1. Digital Marketing Shift → Strategy Over Tactics

**Content:**  
https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/do-die-how-your-online-marketing-can-dump-unnecessary-shrivastava/

**Core Insight:**
Digital marketing success is no longer about presence—it’s about **strategic alignment with audience behavior**.

**Key Ideas:**
- Shift from traditional → digital is already complete
- Channels matter less than **how well they match audience behavior**
- Core pillars:
  - Content + SEO
  - Video marketing
  - Social media engagement
  - Voice search
  - Live interaction

**Strategic Takeaway for LinkedIn:**
- Content must be **intentional, not reactive**
- Random posting = noise
- Strategy = **where + how + why you show up**

**Critical Observation:**
This is foundational—but still somewhat **broad and early-stage thinking** compared to her newer content.

### 2. B2B Has Already Changed (Not “Changing”)

**Content:**  
https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/b2b-isnt-changing-has-already-changed-shraddha-shrivastava-zmd8f/

**Core Insight:**
B2B growth is shifting from **visibility → decision influence**.

**Key Ideas:**

#### a. Vanity Metrics Are Dead
- Impressions ≠ impact
- Reach ≠ revenue

> “If a metric doesn’t move a business decision, it doesn’t deserve your attention.”

#### b. Buyability > Awareness
- Old model: broadcast awareness
- New model: focus on **accounts that actually convert**
- Alignment across:
  - Marketing
  - Sales
  - Finance

#### c. Pre-Sales is Where Deals Are Won
- Decisions happen **before conversations**
- Content shapes:
  - Trust
  - Perception
  - Buyer confidence

#### d. Video = High-Conversion Format
- Not about reach → about **conversation generation**
- Builds **personal connection faster than text**

#### e. AI ≠ Replacement
- AI supports execution
- But:
  - Human-led thinking = core driver
  - Automation ≠ advantage (yet)

#### f. Brand + Performance Must Coexist
- Performance → revenue
- Brand → memory

→ Sustainable growth requires both
