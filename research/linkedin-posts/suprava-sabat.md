## Extracting Leads from Competitor Audiences
Link:
https://www.linkedin.com/posts/priyankas111_how-to-steal-your-competitors-leads-ugcPost-7426920357074321409-vPGg

### Key Insights

- Commenters = high-intent leads
  → People engaging on posts (especially about scaling) signal active interest  
  → More valuable than passive followers

- Engagement mining > audience building alone
  → Instead of growing your own audience first, leverage existing ones  
  → Competitor/influencer audiences become acquisition channels

- Intent-based segmentation is critical
  → Leads categorized by engagement level (high/medium/low)  
  → Prioritization improves outreach efficiency

- Automation enables scale
  → Tools scrape, organize, and enrich lead data quickly  
  → Removes manual bottlenecks

- Direct bridge from content → outreach
  → Engagement is immediately converted into outbound sequences

  ## Multichannel Lead Generation Without Ads or Cold Email
Link:
https://www.linkedin.com/posts/suprava-sabat-saasleadgen_inside-of-a-10m-company-that-generates-activity-7302274636468133888-qgyC

### Key Insights

- Standard channels are saturated
  → Cold email, LinkedIn DMs, and ads are overused  
  → Differentiation requires unconventional approaches

- Intent data is the foundation
  → Signals: hiring, funding, company activity  
  → Enables highly targeted outreach

- Pattern interrupt increases response rates
  → Handwritten notes + memes stand out  
  → Breaks predictable outreach patterns

- Multistep, multichannel sequencing
  → Offline (letters) + online (email, memes)  
  → Increases touchpoint diversity

- Structured messaging framework (PAS)
  → Problem → Agitation → Solution  
  → Guides narrative across outreach

- High conversion comes from system design
  → Coordinated channels + timing → improved close rates
