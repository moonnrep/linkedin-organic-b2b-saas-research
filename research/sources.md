## Topic: LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

This document contains 10 high-signal experts actively practicing LinkedIn organic content, B2B SaaS GTM, and demand generation. All sources were collected from real LinkedIn content (posts/articles), with a focus on execution over theory.

---

## Collection Method

- LinkedIn content was **manually scraped and structured** (no official public API available).
- Posts were selected based on **recency (2024–2025)** and **practical GTM relevance**.
- Where applicable, long-form LinkedIn articles were included.
- YouTube transcripts were not prioritized in this dataset due to the **LinkedIn-native nature** of the selected experts, but can be extended later (e.g., podcasts, talks).
